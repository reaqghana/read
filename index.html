Read About anything in Ghana


Ghana , officially the Republic of Ghana, is a country along the Gulf of Guinea and the Atlantic Ocean, in the subregion of West Africa. Many view Ghana as the most peaceful and accessible of West African states, to which they use Ghana as a base to explore other African countries. Living in Ghana is a one-of-a-kind experience despite some drawbacks associated with the country and its systems. Ghana still makes up for its rich culture,friendliness and originality it serves to its visitors.
